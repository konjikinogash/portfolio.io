Design and Analysis of Algorithms

An advanced course exploring the design and analysis of algorithms, including theoretical and practical concepts.

Introduction to Design and Analysis of Algorithms (DAA): Algorithms are the cornerstone of computer science and engineering. They are well-defined, step-by-step computational procedures that take some input and produce a desired output. Understanding how algorithms work and how to design efficient algorithms is critical to solving problems effectively in the real world.

Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) are a global call to action to end poverty, protect the planet, and ensure peace and prosperity for all by 2030.

Key SDGs related to our project:

  • Goal 6: Clean Water and Sanitation - Ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all.
  • Goal 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities - Make cities inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable.
  • Goal 13: Climate Action - Take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts.

Our efforts in Jaipur aim to address these goals by implementing effective water management systems, promoting sustainability, and ensuring equitable access to resources.
