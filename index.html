Design and Analysis of Algorithms

An advanced course exploring the design and analysis of algorithms, including theoretical and practical concepts.

The course dives into algorithmic efficiency and real-world problem-solving methodologies.

About Course

Deep dive into algorithmic techniques and their impact on real-world challenges.

This course encompasses essential topics like:

  • Sorting Techniques (Bubble, Selection, Insertion, Qiuck, Merge, Heap)
  • Graph Traversals and Pathfinding (BFS, DFS,Dijkstra,BellmonFord etc)
  • Advanced Trees (AVL, Red-Black Trees)
  • String Matching and Dynamic Programming

Course Project

The course project addresses a real-world problem, applying algorithmic methods for efficient solutions.

Students are required to showcase their understanding through innovative solutions to real challenges.

Learning Reflections

Reflecting on the core principles of algorithms, including time complexity, recursion, and practical implementations.

Students gain a comprehensive understanding of both theory and application.

This course enhances understanding in:

  • Algorithmic Problem-Solving Techniques (Divide and Conquer, Greedy)
  • Efficiency Analysis (Time-Space Trade-offs)
  • Data Structures and their Applications (Trees, Graphs, Heaps)
